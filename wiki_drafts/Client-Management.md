# Client Management

Efficiently managing your client database saves time and ensures accurate billing.

## ðŸ‘¥ Adding a Client

1.  Go to the **Clients** tab.
2.  Click **+ Add Client**.

### Key Fields
*   **Name**: The legal name of the business or individual.
*   **GSTIN**: Goods and Services Tax Identification Number.
    *   *Validation*: InvoBharat validates the format of the GSTIN.
    *   *Auto-State*: The State is automatically derived from the first 2 digits of the GSTIN (e.g., "27" for Maharashtra).
*   **Address**: Billing address.
*   **Contact**: Email and Phone (useful for directory reference).

## ðŸ“‡ Client Ledger

The **Client Ledger** gives you a financial history of a specific client.
1.  Click on any Client in the list.
2.  Select the **Ledger** view.
3.  You will see a chronological list of:
    *   Invoices raised (Debit).
    *   Payments received (Credit).
    *   **Running Balance**: How much they currently owe you.
