# Dashboard & Analytics

The **Dashboard** is your command center in InvoBharat. It provides a real-time snapshot of your business's financial health, helping you make informed decisions without digging into complex spreadsheets.

At the top of the dashboard, you will see key summary cards:

*   **Total Revenue**: The sum of all *paid* invoices for the selected period.
*   **Total Invoices**: The count of invoices generated.
*   **Tax Liability**: The total GST (CGST + SGST + IGST) collected that you are liable to pay to the government.
*   **Outstanding**: (If applicable) Total amount pending from clients.
