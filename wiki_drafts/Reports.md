# Reports

InvoBharat provides powerful reporting tools to help you analyze your earnings and outstanding payments.

## ðŸ•’ Aging Report

The **Aging Report** is critical for maintaining cash flow. It shows you which clients owe you money and for how long.
1.  Navigate to **Reports** > **Aging**.
2.  The report categorizes receivables into buckets:
    *   **0-30 Days**: Recent invoices.
    *   **31-60 Days**: Overdue by a month.
    *   **61-90 Days**: Overdue by two months.
    *   **90+ Days**: Critically overdue.
3.  Use this to prioritize which clients to follow up with.

Tracks all incoming payments linked to invoices.
*   **Date**: When the payment was received.
*   **Mode**: Cash, Bank Transfer, UPI, Cheque.
*   **Reference**: Transaction ID (if applicable).
*   **Client**: Who paid.
