## ðŸ› ï¸ Initial Setup

When you launch InvoBharat for the first time, you should set up your business profile to ensure your invoices look professional.

### 1. Business Profile Concept
Go to **Settings** > **Business Profile**. Here you will enter the details that appear on the **Header** and **Footer** of your invoices.

### 2. Required Details
- **Business Name**: Your company or shop name.
- **GSTIN**: Your Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (if applicable).
  - *Note: InvoBharat automatically determines Inter-state (IGST) or Intra-state (CGST+SGST) tax based on the first two digits of your GSTIN vs your Client's GSTIN.*
- **Address**: Your full business address.
- **Contact Info**: Phone number and Email (optional but recommended).

### 3. Branding
- **Logo**: Click the placeholder image to upload your business logo.
- **Signature**: Upload a digital signature image to be stamped on invoices.

### 4. Save
Click **Save** to apply these settings. These will now be auto-filled on every new invoice you create.
