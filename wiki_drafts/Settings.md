## ðŸ’¾ Backup & Restore

Your data is stored locally on your device. To prevent data loss (e.g., if you format your PC), you must take regular backups.

### How to Backup
1.  Go to **Settings** > **Backup**.
2.  Click **Create Backup**.
3.  Save the `.db` or `.zip` file to a safe location (e.g., Google Drive, External Hard Drive).

### How to Restore
1.  Go to **Settings** > **Backup**.
2.  Click **Restore**.
3.  Select a previously created backup file.
4.  **Warning**: This will replace your current data with the backup data.
